## Source Types

- **AMFI PDF**: Official scheme information documents from Association of Mutual Funds in India
- **AMFI SSD PDF**: Scheme Specific Documents with detailed fund information
- **SEBI Portal**: Securities and Exchange Board of India official fund details
- **AMC/Groww Websites**: Fund house and distributor websites with scheme information

## Notes

- All sources are official and publicly available
- Data is scraped periodically to ensure accuracy
- The bot uses intelligent AI extraction to identify relevant information from these sources
